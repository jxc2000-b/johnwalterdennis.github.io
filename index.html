Why?

I wanted to make a website that felt simple and personal, in strong opposition to corporate platforms like Linkedin, this is my attempt at that. I understand that not everyone will like this format, and that is okay, all I want to do is present an alternative, you should get to choose how you want to represent yourself online. I also think that multimedia is the most effective way to communicate who I actually am as a person, text alone is pretty boring, this seems like where things in general also seem to be heading.
